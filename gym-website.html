Our Facilities

WORLD-CLASS
EQUIPMENT

Over 18,000 sq ft of premium training space, equipped with the latest gear from Rogue, Life Fitness, and Eleiko. Every zone engineered for peak performance.

01 🏋️
Strength Floor
200+ free weights, power racks, deadlift platforms, and dedicated Olympic lifting stations with competition-grade bumper plates.
02 🚴
Cardio Zone
100 cardio stations — treadmills, assault bikes, ski ergs, rowers, and stair climbers with integrated entertainment screens.

Full boxing ring, heavy bags, speed bags, and an open mat area for MMA, BJJ, and kickboxing training sessions.

Fuel Bar
On-site nutrition bar with protein shakes, whole food meals, supplements, and a registered dietitian on staff daily.
